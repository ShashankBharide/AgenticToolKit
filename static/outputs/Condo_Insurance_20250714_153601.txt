### FAQs

**Q: What does condo insurance typically cover?**
A: Condo insurance usually covers personal belongings, liability protection, additional living expenses, and improvements made to the unit.

**Q: How is condo insurance different from home insurance?**
A: Condo insurance typically covers the interior of the unit, while home insurance covers both the interior and exterior of a standalone house.

**Q: Can I lower my condo insurance premiums?**
A: Yes, you can potentially lower your premiums by installing security features, bundling policies, or increasing your deductible.

**Q: Do I need condo insurance if my condo association has a master policy?**
