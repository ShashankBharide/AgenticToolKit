## Frequently Asked Questions About Classic Car Insurance

**1. What qualifies a car as a classic for insurance purposes?**
To qualify for classic car insurance, most insurers stipulate the vehicle to be at least 25 years old, utilized for pleasure driving, and well-maintained.

**2. Can I use my classic car for everyday driving with classic car insurance?**
Most classic car insurance policies impose restrictions on mileage and usage, necessitating clarity on your intended usage with your insurer.

**3. Are spare parts covered under classic car insurance policies?**
Certain insurers offer optional spare parts coverage, ensuring seamless repairs or restoration of your classic car.

**4. How is the value of a classic car determined for insurance purposes?**
Classic car insurance policies typically employ agreed value or appraised value to ascertain the vehicle's worth in case of a claim.

**5. Can I get discounts on classic car insurance if I have multiple classic cars insured?**
Numerous insurers extend multi-car discounts to classic car enthusiasts with more than one cherished vehicle.
