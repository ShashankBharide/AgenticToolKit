## Coverage Options for Nonprofit Business Insurance in Ontario

When considering nonprofit business insurance in Ontario, it's essential to understand the different types of coverage available. Here are some key coverage options that nonprofit organizations can benefit from:

- **Commercial General Liability:** Protects your nonprofit from liability claims related to property damage or bodily injury.
- **Non-Owned Automobile Liability:** Safeguards your organization in case of legal action resulting from a vehicle accident involving personal vehicles used for business purposes.
- **Non-Profit Directors and Officers Liability:** Provides protection for directors and officers against legal actions and financial losses.
- **Errors and Omissions Liability:** Covers claims of financial loss due to errors or omissions in your services.
- **Umbrella Liability:** Offers additional liability coverage beyond the limits of your primary policies to protect against major lawsuits.
