## Understanding Errors and Omissions Insurance

Errors and Omissions insurance is specifically designed to protect professionals and businesses from claims of inadequate work, negligence, misrepresentation, or breach of duty. It goes beyond the coverage provided by general liability insurance, which typically focuses on property damage, bodily injury, and personal injury claims.

### Key Features of Errors and Omissions Insurance:

1. **Defense Coverage**: E&O insurance provides coverage for legal defense costs in case a client files a claim against your business.
  
2. **Specific Representation**: Tailored coverage options to meet the unique needs of your business.
  
3. **Flexible Limits**: Customizable coverage limits to ensure adequate protection for your business.
  
4. **Additional Defense Coverage**: Supplementary coverage for disciplinary proceedings or legal actions.

### Who Needs Errors and Omissions Insurance?

Professionals across various industries in Ontario, Canada can benefit from Errors and Omissions insurance. Whether you are a bookkeeper, mediator, data processor, travel agent, or accountant, if your work involves providing services, advice, or consulting to clients, E&O insurance is essential.
