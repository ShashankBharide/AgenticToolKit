## Types of RV Insurance Coverage

### Liability Coverage

Liability coverage protects you in case you are at fault in an accident that causes injury or property damage to others. This coverage is essential for RV owners in Ontario to comply with legal requirements and safeguard their financial assets.

### Collision and Comprehensive Coverage

Collision coverage helps pay for damages to your RV resulting from an accident with another vehicle or object, while comprehensive coverage protects against non-collision events such as theft, vandalism, or natural disasters. These coverages ensure that your RV is protected in various scenarios.

### Contents Coverage

Contents coverage protects your personal belongings inside the RV, such as electronics, appliances, and camping gear. In the event of theft or damage, this coverage helps replace or repair your belongings, allowing you to continue enjoying your travels without disruptions.
