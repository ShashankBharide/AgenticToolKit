## Why Contractor Insurance is Crucial

### 1. Protection in Disputes
Contractor liability insurance offers you recourse in case of disputes with clients. Whether it's a disagreement over project deliverables or an incident involving a worker, having insurance coverage can help mitigate the financial risks associated with civil lawsuits.

### 2. Completed Operations Coverage
With completed operations coverage, your policy can protect you if issues arise post-project completion, leading to customer injuries or property damage. This coverage ensures that you are shielded from liabilities that may arise in the future.

### 3. Comprehensive Coverage
Contractor insurance not only covers general liabilities but also offers protection for property damage, business interruptions, and equipment failures. By investing in comprehensive coverage, you can safeguard your assets and maintain business continuity.
